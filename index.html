✦ About This Project ✦

Why Trail For Wanderers?

India is not a destination — it is a thousand destinations wrapped in one ancient, vibrant, ever-evolving land. Trail For Wanderers is our engineering project built to make that richness accessible to every curious traveller.

We cover all 28 states and 8 Union Territories with weather info, best-season guides, famous landmarks, street food, hotel picks, and a helpful tour guide chatbot.
